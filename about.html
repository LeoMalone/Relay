ABOUT US



Relay Academy is a Toronto-based consultancy specializing in Salesforce Marketing Cloud implementation, technical strategy, campaign management, and data-driven journeys.
We’ve helped clients:

• Deliver upward of 1 billion emails per month

• Develop business requirements for highly complex, dynamic programs (upward of 1500 possible versions)

• Stand up their new SFMC platform

• Create process documentation and requirements packages to serve individual client needs

• Train their senior leadership and execution teams on end to end campaign management processes and best practices so they can confidently operate their own SFMC platform

• Adopt CRM and journey practices and uncover personalization opportunities that drive engagement and loyalty



CRM is where individuality shines and we pride ourselves on finding unique solutions fitting for unique client needs.
We believe that the only way to succeed is by being curious, honing our listening skills, and being good partners to our clients.
